SSM Training Services LTD.

SSM Global TRaining services is a multi-discipline learning centre founded in 2012 to provide education and training for adults in Larnaca Region of Cyprus.

The main function of SSM Global Training services is to provide initial and continuing vocational training and education for adults. Vocational adult education can be self-motivated further education, or organised as employment or in-service training programmes, or based on apprenticeship contracts.

Training and education cover school-based, institute level, even to higher vocational level. Many training programmes, conducted to achieve external certificates and diplomas from Cambridge or Edexcel universities leading to formal qualifications: basic, advanced or specialised qualifications.

The SSM Global TRaining services of Continuing Education offers a large and flexible programme of part-time learning opportunities for adults. The SSM Global TRaining services is committed both to the highest standards of excellence, and to ensuring that the unparalleled academic strengths and resources are made available to as many interested learners as possible.
